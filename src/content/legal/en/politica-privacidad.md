Your data will be stored for the following periods:

After the deadline, we will securely delete or anonymise the data.

To exercise these rights, send a request to **info@alcvaletparking.com**, indicating "Exercise of RGPD rights" in the subject line and attaching a copy of your DNI/NIE.
