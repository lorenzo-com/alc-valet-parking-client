## 1. INTRODUCTION

At ALC VALET PARKING we use cookies and similar technologies to improve the user experience, offer personalised content and analyse website traffic. By continuing to browse our website www.alcvaletparking.com, you accept the use of cookies in the terms described in this policy.

## 2. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, mobile, tablet) when you visit a website. These files allow the website to remember information about your visit, such as language preferences, logged in session or viewing settings, in order to improve your browsing experience.

## 3. TYPES OF COOKIES WE USE

### 3.1. According to their goal:

- **Technical or necessary cookies:** These enable the website to function and allow access to basic functions, such as login or website security.
- **Personalisation cookies:** These store your preferences, such as language or the appearance of the site.
- **Analytical or performance cookies:** These help us to understand how users interact with the website in order to improve its performance.
- **Advertising and marketing cookies:** These are used to display personalised advertisements based on your browsing habits.

### 3.2. Depending on their duration:

- **Session cookies:** These are automatically deleted when the browser is closed.
- **Persistent cookies:** They remain on your device for a certain period of time before being automatically deleted.

### 3.3. Depending on who manages them:

- **Own cookies:** Managed by ALC VALET PARKING to ensure the proper functioning of the site.
- **Third-party cookies:** Managed by external services (e.g. Google Analytics, Facebook, etc.) that may collect data on the use of the website.
